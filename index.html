What we're
about at
Belmat brothers?

BBF is a non-profit charitable with a social life works,committed to providing support to underpriviledged people in Nigeria.

Established in 2018, the belmat brothers foundation has the vision to bring Nigerians out of poverty by improving the quality of life of a person in need.
We are helping them to have hope for a better future.
Belmat brothers foundation is not politically motivated despite founders being from political family.

Success stories

Foundation donates to 15,000 Zamfara households

The belmat brothers foundation distributed palliatives, funds and essential items to over 15,000 household across the 14 local governments area of Zamfara state, due to the covid-19 pandemic. Items distributed includes, Rice, Garri, Beans, Groundnut oil, Dried catfish, tomatoe paste and cash.

Shared relief materials to displaced families

On a service to humanity, the belmat brothers distributed relief materials to displaced families in Hayin Bawa Danjalbi-Maru where people from akuzo, Bawar Daji and Zanoka are taking refuge.

Donated items to internally displaced in Bungudu

The belmat brothers foundation assisted internally displaced persons in Bungudu, Maru and other areas of Zamfara by donating food items to them.
